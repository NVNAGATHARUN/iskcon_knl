Programs at ISKCON Kurnool

Connect with Krishna through Classes, Kirtan & Community Service

Sunday Feast Program

The Sunday Feast is a weekly festival featuring kirtan, discourse on Bhagavad-gita or Srimad-Bhagavatam, and delicious Krishna-prasadam for all visitors. This is the perfect way to experience the warmth of our spiritual community.

  • Day: Every Sunday
  • Time: 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM
  • Includes: Kirtan, Lecture, Arati & Prasadam
  • Cost: Free for all (families & newcomers welcome)

Join the Feast

Experience Krishna consciousness with our spiritual family every Sunday.
