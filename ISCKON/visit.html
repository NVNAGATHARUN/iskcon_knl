Sunday Feast Program

Every Sunday, ISKCON Kurnool conducts a special evening program featuring kirtan, discourse on Bhagavad-gita or Srimad-Bhagavatam, and delicious Krishna-prasadam. All are welcome!

Day

Every Sunday

Time

5:00 PM – 8:00 PM (approx)

Cost

Free for all

Visitor Guidelines

Do's
  • Dress modestly and respectfully
  • Remove shoes before entering temple
  • Maintain silence in the temple hall
  • Participate respectfully in kirtan
  • Accept prasadam with gratitude
Don'ts
  • No non-vegetarian items in temple
  • No intoxicants or tobacco
  • No mobile phones in loud mode
  • Photography only with permission
  • Do not point feet towards deities

Important Information

Dress Code: Wear clean, modest clothing. Women should prefer sarees or long dresses. Men should wear dhotis or long pants.

Photography: Photography may be restricted during certain aratis. Always ask temple authorities before taking photos.

First Visit: Newcomers are always welcome! Approach any devotee for guidance on darshan etiquette.

Prasadam: Accept prasadam with your right hand and eat with gratitude. Do not waste or disrespect food.
