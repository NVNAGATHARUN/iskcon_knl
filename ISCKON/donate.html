Donate & Support Seva

Your Contribution Helps Spread Krishna Consciousness

Why Support ISKCON Kurnool?

ISKCON temples maintain daily deity worship, distributes free prasadam, conducts spiritual education, and serve the community. Your generous donations help us continue this sacred service. Even a small offering made with devotion becomes invaluable in Krishna's eyes.

Types of Seva (Service)

Annadan / Prasadam Seva

Support the distribution of sanctified vegetarian food (prasadam) to visitors, devotees and needy people.

Donation Options:

• Sponsor one day's prasadam
• Sponsor weekly Sunday feast
• Monthly prasadam sponsorship
• Any amount of your choice
Deity Worship Seva

Contribute towards bhoga (food offerings), flowers, dresses and decorations of Sri Sri Jagannath, Baladeva, Subhadra and Sri Sri Gaura Nitai.

Donation Options:

• Daily deity seva
• Weekly worship sponsorship
• Festival decorations
• Dress & ornament fund
Temple & Outreach Seva

Help maintain the temple premises, support preaching programs, books distribution and community outreach activities.

Donation Options:

• Temple maintenance
• Books & literature fund
• Preaching programs
• General donation
