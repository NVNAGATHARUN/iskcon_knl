Their Lordships at ISKCON Kurnool

The worship of the Deities is the heart of ISKCON temples, performed according to the standards given by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada.

Presiding Deities

Sri Sri Jagannath Baladeva Subhadra

The merciful Lord of the universe, Sri Jagannath, along with His brother Baladeva and sister Subhadra, bestows their blessings upon the devotees of Kurnool through Their beautiful darshan.

Sri Sri Gaura Nitai

Lord Chaitanya Mahaprabhu and Lord Nityananda are the most compassionate incarnation of the Lord, freely distributing the chanting of the holy names in this age of Kali.

Darshan & Altar

Devotees can have darshan of the Deities during the temple's regular darshan hours and arati timings, accompanied by kirtan and devotional atmosphere. Please refer to the Visit page for detailed timings.
